Ingredients to make Hyderabadi Chicken Dum Briyani

Fried onions

  • 1 lb onions yellow, finely sliced
  • 1.5 cups, Peanut oil

Chicken Marination

  • 1½ lb chicken with bone, cut and clean
  • ⅔ cup yogurt plain
  • 1½ tablespoon ginger garlic paste or grated ginger and garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt adjust per taste
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric ground

Briyani Spices

  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamon
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp shah Jeera
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Briyani

  • 2 cups basmati rice long grain
  • Salt as per taste
  • 5 tbsp Oil
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp ghee, optional
  • 1 tsp saffron, dissolved in 2 tsp hot water

Spices for rice

  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamon
  • 5 cloves
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp shah Jeera
